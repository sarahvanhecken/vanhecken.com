About

I recently did the math (and I try to avoid that as much as I can) and came to the conclusion I’ve been working in the digital field for 13 years. Thirteen internet years, that must be like 87 in human years right? It can sure feel like it.

Clearly a lot has changed over the years. I started out as a digital designer and even build (ha!) some of my own websites. Later on I became an Art Director and then for a

couple of years the Creative Director of a friend of mine, formerly a small cool agency from Amsterdam that is now part of international agency Dept.

At Dept I learned a lot about marketing and started to really use the strategic part of my brain.

In between all those things some other stuff happend, but I’m running out of paragraph. Let’s talk!

Projects

Advertising kit for C.T.A.

Role: Creative strategy for Dept Agency

Production of a set of materials to help CTA (the Technical Centre for Agricultural and Rural Co-operation) communicate. We went on a week long mission to Uganda to gather materials for the kit that is based on photography, film, illustrations and text.

Storytelling for Microsoft

Role: Creative direction for Dept Agency

Microsoft Netherlands asked us to create a longread story on the impact of technology. What it can do for society, for companies but more importantly: for humans. We turned the story into a seven chapter webpage. And got out of our offices, onto the road.

Digital platform for DELA

Role: Digital C.D at a friend of mine

As a seller of life insurances, Cooperation DELA knows how to talk about life and death. Literally. This campaign centred around traditions and life lessons. Those ideas passed along by parents or grandparents. Because that what you pass on, will not be lost.

View the work

Campaign for Royal Philips

Role: C.D at a friend of mine

Digital PR campaign for Royal Philips. They rebranded and wanted to generate buzz. We created a website where people could claim a pixel that would uncover a bit of the logo. The campaign went viral: with zero media budget we created over 375.000 in media value.
